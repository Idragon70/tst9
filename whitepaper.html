WW3 - Ward War 3 Cryptocurrency

Purpose

WW3 is a limited‑supply, mineable cryptocurrency created to symbolize global nuclear deterrence. Capped at 20 million coins, it reflects the symbolic total number of nuclear warheads on Earth.

Mining Algorithm

WW3 uses a custom hybrid mining algorithm that supports GPU, CPU, M.2 NVMe SSD, and RAM‑based proof‑of‑load mechanism.

Distribution Timeline

Distributed over a 10‑year mining plan, symbolizing a decade of global nuclear stability through deterrence.

Security & Symbolism

The name WW3 is not a threat but a signal: the more we recognize destructive power, the more we strive for peace through technology.
